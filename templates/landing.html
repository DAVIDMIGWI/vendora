How It Works

Getting started with Vendora is simple. Follow these easy steps to start ordering or selling.

For Buyers

1

Create Account

Sign up as a buyer with your email and phone number. It takes less than a minute!

2

Browse & Add to Cart

Explore local vendors and their products. Add items to your cart from multiple vendors.

3

Place Order

Enter your delivery address and place your order. Vendor will set the delivery fee.

4

Pay & Receive

Pay when your order arrives. Cash on delivery or follow vendor's payment instructions.

For Vendors

1

Register Your Shop

Create a vendor account with your shop details. Wait for admin approval.

2

Add Products

Upload your products with images, prices, and descriptions. Organize by categories.

3

Accept Orders

Receive orders from buyers. Set delivery fees and manage unavailable items.

4

Deliver & Get Paid

Update order status, deliver to customers, and confirm payments received.
